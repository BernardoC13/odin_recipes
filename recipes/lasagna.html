Lasagna

Description

This is my favorite lasagna recipe ever. From Italy itself, this recipe was passed down generations of a Chefs family. It's cheesy and you can feel the flavour of the tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Step-by-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
